About Us

We are an independent locally-owned and operated upscale restaurant and have been open continuously since 1947. Shaw’s is currently undergoing renovation. We will tentatively reopen in early July, 2017 into three concepts. Shaw’s will continue as a fine dining restaurant. The bar will be moved within the building and renamed Harry’s Bar (after Harry Shaw the original owner). The previous bar space will become a bakery named Mable & Grace. We are looking to hire experienced and qualified team members that are serious about a career in the restaurant industry.

We are open every day except Christmas Day and New Year’s Day. We are open all Holidays and working on those days is mandatory for all staff. On Sundays, Shaw’s and Harry’s will tentatively close at 3 pm and the Bakery will close at 5 pm.
